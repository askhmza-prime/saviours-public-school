About Saviour's Public School

Serving the Civil Lines community with quality education

Our Story

A Cornerstone of Civil Lines Education

Saviour's Public School stands as one of the most trusted educational institutions in the Civil Lines area of Moradabad. Located conveniently on SBI Road, near the District Court, the school has become a familiar and respected landmark for local residents.

We provide foundational education from Grades 1 through 8, with a comprehensive curriculum that includes academics, arts, and physical education — ensuring every child receives a truly holistic development experience.

With a strong 4.4-star rating from over 85 community reviews, our reputation speaks for itself. Parents consistently praise our cooperative staff, spacious classrooms, and the warm school environment that makes every child feel safe and valued.
