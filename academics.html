Academics

Curriculum

Classes 1 to 8

A well-rounded curriculum including academics, arts, and physical education — designed for holistic development of every child.

1️⃣

Class 1 & 2

Foundation learning — Hindi, basic English, Maths, and EVS through activity-based methods.

3️⃣

Class 3 & 4

Building on basics — reading, writing, arithmetic, and environmental science deepened.

5️⃣

Class 5 & 6

Expanding knowledge — Science, Social Science, Hindi, Maths introduced at greater depth.

7️⃣

Class 7 & 8

Upper primary — preparing students for secondary education with strong conceptual clarity.

Subjects

Core Subjects

📖

Hindi

Primary medium — grammar, literature, writing, and comprehension for all classes.

🔢

Mathematics

Arithmetic, geometry, and problem solving — building strong numerical foundations.

🔬

Science / EVS

Environmental studies to Science — practical approach to understanding the natural world.

🌍

Social Science

History, Geography, and Civics — developing awareness of society and the world.

🔤

English

English language — reading, writing, and basic communication skills for all grades.

💻

Computer Education

Digital literacy through our computer lab — 6 functional computers for hands-on learning.
